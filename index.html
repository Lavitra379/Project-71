School Technological Acheivements

Scan ID
Introduced ID cards scanning through machienes

An e-I Card system is installed in DON BOSCO ENGLISH SCHOOL This system takes care of the attendance system of calling roll number of every student, which is highly time consuming every day as well as to prepare reports at the end of the year. ScaniD also helps in recording the in and out time of a particular student. It also creates a parent information system e.g. everyday system sends a SMS to all the parents of Primary and Secondary section students, whose children are absent for the day. Also special SMS’s can be sent to parents in case of emergency holiday.

Digital Classroom
Introduced Digital Classrooms in the school

Don Bosco English School was the first to implement Digital Classroom i.e. E-learning concept in Badlapur. It consists of a dependable hardware along with most comprehensive and engaging content, custom-made for Indian students in each classroom. The content is based on a firm belief that learning is not a passive process and that better learning takes place when learners are actively involved in the process. The content is updated according to changes in syllabus and student feedback is also duly taken into consideration
